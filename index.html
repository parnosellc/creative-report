Creative Performance — US vs ROW

Video ads split by funnel (FCVSL vs Toolkit) and country (US vs rest-of-world), both accounts combined, 1-day-click. Top spenders + up-n-comers for each of the 8 segments, plus a non-video catch-all (US+ROW combined). Ad names link into Ads Manager.

Default window is the last 14 days. Widen the dates and re-run for longer periods. Dates use each ad account's time zone. Note: the "created in last (days)" filter applies only to the up-n-comer tables — top-spender tables and the catch-all section ignore ad age.
Default is the FB pixel purchase. Switch to your Hyros custom conversion to attribute on that — events are sorted by volume with counts shown. Changing it updates every table instantly.
